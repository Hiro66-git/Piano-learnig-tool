## 🎼 Common Scales You Can Play

### C Major Scale
Press in sequence: **A S D F G H J K**
(Notes: C D E F G A B C)

### Natural Minor Scale
Press in sequence: **A S E F G U J K**
(Notes: C D Eb F G Ab B C)

### Pentatonic Scale (5 notes)
Press in sequence: **A S D G H**
(Notes: C D E G A)

## 📍 Octave Indicator

Your current octave is shown in the app. When you change octaves with **Z** or **X**, the same keys produce different notes:

```
Z (lower)          Current Octave          X (raise)
   ↓                                           ↑
Octave 3        Octave 4 (default)        Octave 5
 Range              A-K keys               Range
C3 to C4          C4 to C5              C5 to C6
 Lower            Middle                 Higher
 Pitch            Pitch                  Pitch
```

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Mistake:** Pressing a key and expecting it to change pitch
✓ **Solution:** Use Z/X to change octaves first, THEN press the same key

❌ **Mistake:** Playing black keys without black keys visible
✓ **Solution:** Enable "Show Labels" in settings to see all notes

❌ **Mistake:** Recording with sound off
✓ **Solution:** Check volume in Settings before pressing R

❌ **Mistake:** Expecting metronome to click at 0% volume
✓ **Solution:** After pressing SPACE, ensure volume > 0%

## 🎓 Learning Tips with Keyboard

### Level 1: Memorization
- Play each key and say the note name aloud (A=C, S=D, etc.)
- Repeat until muscle memory forms (~5-10 minutes)

### Level 2: Scales
- Play C Major scale 3x (A S D F G H J K)
- Move to other octaves using Z/X
- Practice daily

### Level 3: Chords
- Learn the three shapes (Major, Minor, etc.)
- Practice switching between chords
- Play with metronome for rhythm

### Level 4: Melodies
- Pick simple songs from the Songs section
- Follow the displayed notes
- Use recording feature to track progress
